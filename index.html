About Me

I’m Likhith K R, a passionate and purpose-driven Computer Science Engineering student at KVG College of Engineering, Sullia. What began as a love for logic and design has grown into a deep commitment to building impactful technology that solves real-world problems.

My curiosity has led me to explore cutting-edge domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Natural Language Processing, IoT, and Full-Stack Web Development. I enjoy taking on challenges that combine creativity with critical thinking.

Some of the projects I'm most proud of include a Multilingual AI Voice Assistant for Farmers, designed to support rural communities with voice/text communication in Kannada, Hindi, and English, and a Mind-Controlled Wheelchair powered by EEG signals, currently in development. I’ve also worked on a Healthcare Appointment System to simplify access to medical professionals, and a client-based Real Estate Portal (Salaga) tailored to property listings and dynamic filtering.

I believe technology should be ethical, inclusive, and purpose-driven. My long-term vision is to become a scientist at ISRO and work on futuristic systems—such as embedded safety devices—to reduce crime and enhance human life.

Outside of development, I love sketching, playing chess, and exploring the deeper mechanics of how systems work—from neural networks to human behavior.
